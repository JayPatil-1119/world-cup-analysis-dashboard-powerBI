# world-cup-analysis-dashboard-powerBI
##1. Problem Statement

World Cup cricket generates massive amounts of match and player-level data, but key challenges include:

Difficulty understanding overall tournament performance at a glance

No clear comparison of batting performances across players and teams

Limited visibility into bowling impact and wicket-taking ability

Lack of a consolidated view to identify top-performing players

These challenges make it hard to evaluate performance and extract meaningful insights quickly.

2. Objective

The objective of this project is to develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that:

Provides a high-level overview of the World Cup

Analyzes batting performance to identify top run scorers and consistency

Evaluates bowling performance to highlight key wicket-takers

Enables quick comparison across teams and players

Presents insights through clean, intuitive visuals

3. Dashboard Structure & Pages
1️⃣ Overview Page

Summary of matches, teams, runs, wickets, and outcomes

Team-wise performance comparison

Overall tournament trends and dominance

2️⃣ Batting Performance Page

Identification of top run scorers

Comparison of batting contributions across players

Highlights consistency and impact in scoring runs

3️⃣ Bowling Performance Page

Analysis of top wicket-takers

Team-wise bowling strength comparison

Insights into bowlers who made crucial breakthroughs

4. Observations & Key Insights
Overall Tournament Insights

Certain teams clearly dominated the tournament in terms of runs and match performance

Performance trends show a clear gap between top-performing and lower-performing teams

Batting Performance Insights

A few batsmen stand out as consistent high run scorers

Strong batting contributions played a key role in team success

Some teams relied heavily on top-order performance

Bowling Performance Insights

Top bowlers made a significant impact through regular wicket-taking

Successful teams showed a balanced bowling attack

Wicket-taking ability proved crucial in controlling matches
